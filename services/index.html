Services

Data Analyses

We focus on making data useful and practical. We organize, review, and interpret information to uncover patterns, identify challenges, and highlight opportunities. Our analysis provides the clarity businesses need to make informed decisions with confidence.

Services include:

  • Descriptive Analysis – turning raw data into clear summaries and reports.

  • Trend & Performance Reviews – spotting changes over time and highlighting strengths or gaps.

  • Root Cause Checks – examining the underlying reasons behind challenges.

  • Decision Support – preparing insights that guide strategies, planning, and improvements.

Data Annotation

Data annotation is the process of labeling and categorizing information so AI models can learn to understand it. This step is essential for ensuring that models make accurate predictions and perform reliably. The quality and precision of annotation directly determine how effective an AI system can be.

Our Data Annotation services include:

  • Image Labeling – bounding boxes, polygons, classification, and segmentation.

  • Text Annotation – entity recognition, sentiment tagging, and document categorization.

  • Audio Transcription & Tagging – speech-to-text, speaker identification, and intent labeling.

  • Video Annotation – object tracking, activity recognition, and frame-by-frame tagging.

Transcription

Accurate transcription makes spoken content searchable, shareable, and ready for analysis. From meetings and interviews to audio datasets for AI, we provide high-quality transcriptions that capture every word with clarity.

Our Transcription services include:

  • Audio Transcription – converting voice recordings into accurate text.

  • Video Transcription – subtitles and captions for accessibility and content reuse.

  • Multilingual Support – transcription services in multiple languages.

  • AI Training Datasets – structured transcripts tailored for machine learning models.
